What is Fountain Prime School? +
Fountain Prime Schools School is a co- educational institution located in Apa,Badagry West Local government Development Area, Lagos State, offering comprehensive education from Nursery through Primary to college levels. Our school is dedicated to nurturing young minds with a blend of academic excellence, moral values, and global awareness. We provide a stimulating learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential, develop critical thinking skills, and achieve personal growth.
At Fountain Prime Schools, we are committed to fostering a community of lifelong learners who are prepared to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision +
Our vision at Fountain Prime Schools is to be a leading educational institution recognized for fostering excellence in academics, innovation, and character.
We aspire to cultivate a generation of well-rounded, confident, and socially responsible individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel in an ever-changing global society.
One of our strongest vision we nurtures everyday is to become number one choice for both parents, learners and staff where undiluted education is prioritized.
Our Mission +
At Fountain Prime Schools, our mission is to provide a holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and global citizenship.
We are committed to creating an inclusive and supportive learning environment where every student is empowered to reach their full potential, develop a love for lifelong learning, and become responsible, compassionate leaders who contribute positively to their communities and the world.
